Game Rules & Points System

⚽ Points Calculation

To keep predictions competitive, we use a weighted point system that rewards picking the underdog. If your prediction is correct, your points for that match are calculated as:

Points = (Total votes on incorrect options) + 1

If your prediction is incorrect, you get 0 points.

⚙️ Match Types

League Stage

3 voting options: Home Win, Away Win, or Draw.

Knockout Stage (KO)

2 voting options: Home Win or Away Win (includes extra time/penalties outcome).

💡 Points Scenarios

Scenario A (Unanimous Favorite Wins):
10 people vote for Argentina, 0 vote for France. Argentina wins.
Points awarded to Argentina voters: 0 + 1 = 1 point each.
Scenario B (Underdog Wins):
10 people vote for Argentina, 2 people vote for France. France wins.
Points awarded to France voters: 10 + 1 = 11 points each.
Scenario C (Three-way League Split):
5 vote Home, 3 vote Draw, 2 vote Away. The match ends in a Draw.
Points awarded to Draw voters: (5 + 2) + 1 = 8 points each.

🔒 Transparency & Privacy

  • Voting is secret: Before a match starts, you cannot see what others predicted or even the number of votes. This prevents copying predictions.
  • Live banter: Once a match kicks off, the voting closes. Predictions are locked, and the app immediately reveals who everyone voted for so you can banter in your group chat.
